About

Ontichuka!

This page is a searchable dictionary for the Alabama language, a Muskogean Language spoken by the Alabama people of the Alabama-Coushatta tribe of Texas and formerly spoken in the Alabama-Quassarte Tribal Town in Oklahoma.

The Alabama Dictionary Online is the result of a collaborative project between the Alabama-Coushatta tribe of Texas and the WOLF (Working on Language in the Field) Lab at Harvard University.

This dictionary was made to document and share lexical and grammatical information for the Alabama language. This site contains a searchable database for words in either English or Alabama: the same search bar will handle either. This website will soon include explanations of various aspects of Alabama grammar.

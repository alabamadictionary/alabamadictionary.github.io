Personal Pronouns

Personal pronouns are used to refer to people or things. In Alabama, personal pronouns can change depending on who is they refer to (me, you, a friend, etc.), how many people they refer to, and what role they are playing in the sentence. Linguists call these traits person, number, and case.

Basic (Bare/Caseless) Personal Pronouns

Linguistics Terminology English Alabama
1st Person Singular I ana
2nd Person Singular You (singular) isna
3rd Person Singular He/She/It ibisna
1st Person Plural We kosna / posna
2st Person Plural Y'all hasna
3rd Person Plural They ibisna
